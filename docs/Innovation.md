# **Innovation Features** 

## **1. Graph-Driven Fraud Intelligence**

* **The Concept**: Fraud in gig insurance is often *coordinated (rings)*, not isolated incidents.
* **Innovation**: Use Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) to model relationships between workers, locations, devices, and claims to detect hidden fraud networks.
* **Impact**: Enables *early detection of large-scale fraud attacks*, preventing systemic payout losses.


## **2. NLP-Based Curfew Detection (BERT)**

* **The Concept**: Disruptions like curfews or strikes are often first reported in unstructured text (news, social media).
* **Innovation**: Use BERT-based NLP models to extract and classify disruption signals from real-time text sources.
* **Impact**: Detects *non-weather disruptions faster than APIs*, improving trigger accuracy.


## **3. Predictive Time-Series Forecasting**

* **The Concept**: Disruptions follow patterns (weather cycles, peak traffic hours).
* **Innovation**: Apply time-series models to forecast future disruption probability at a hyper-local level.
* **Impact**: Enables *proactive risk management* and smarter premium adjustments.


## **4. Multi-Source Cross-Validation**

* **The Concept**: Single-source data (e.g., GPS or weather API) is vulnerable to spoofing or inaccuracies.
* **Innovation**: Cross-validate signals across weather APIs, GPS, traffic data, and device telemetry.
* **Impact**: Ensures *high data integrity* and reduces false claims.


## **5. Worker Reliability Factor**

* **The Concept**: Not all workers exhibit the same behavioral patterns or risk levels.
* **Innovation**: Build a reliability score based on historical activity, claim behavior, and consistency.
* **Impact**: Enables *fair pricing and fraud-resistant claims processing*.


## **6. Dynamic Premium Adjustment**

* **The Concept**: Risk is not static — it fluctuates based on real-time conditions.
* **Innovation**: Continuously adjust weekly premiums using live disruption risk and worker profile.
* **Impact**: Creates a *fair, adaptive pricing model* aligned with real-world risk.


## **7. Multi-Platform Activity Aggregation**

* **The Concept**: Gig workers operate across multiple platforms simultaneously.
* **Innovation**: Aggregate active hours across apps (Zomato, Swiggy, Zepto, etc.) using device-level signals.
* **Impact**: Calculates *true income loss*, not limited to a single platform.


## **8. “Earnings-at-Risk” Dashboard**

* **The Concept**: Workers lack visibility into potential income loss during disruptions.
* **Innovation**: Real-time dashboard showing protected income vs. at-risk income based on current conditions.
* **Impact**: Empowers workers with *data-driven decision making*.


## **9. “Safety Credits” (No-Claim Rewards)**

* **The Concept**: Encouraging safer work patterns and long-term engagement.
* **Innovation**: Reward workers with credits if they operate in risky conditions without claims.
* **Impact**: Improves *retention and reduces unnecessary claims*.

## **10. Community Proof of Disruption**

* **The Concept**: Micro-level disruptions are often missed by centralized systems.
* **Innovation**: Use crowd-sourced validation from nearby workers to confirm disruption events.
* **Impact**: Provides *real-time ground truth* and strengthens fraud detection.
